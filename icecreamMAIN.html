Vanilla Ice-Cream

Ingradients:-

Certainly! Here are the ingredients typically used to make chole bhature, a popular sweet dish:

For Ice-Cream:

2 cups heavy cream 1 cup whole milk 3/4 cup granulated sugar 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract Pinch of salt These ingredients are mixed together, then churned and frozen to create delicious and creamy vanilla ice cream. You can also customize your vanilla ice cream by adding ingredients like chocolate chips, fruit, or nuts if desired.

Straberry Ice-Cream

Ingradients:-

Certainly! Here are the ingredients typically used to make Kadai Paneer, a popular Indian sweet dish:

For the straberry ice-cream:

2 cups fresh or frozen strawberries, hulled and sliced 1 cup granulated sugar 2 cups heavy cream 1 cup whole milk 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract (optional) Pinch of salt These ingredients are blended together with the strawberries to create a strawberry puree, and then mixed with the cream, milk, sugar, and optional vanilla extract. The mixture is then churned and frozen to make delicious strawberry ice cream. Enjoy!

Mint Chocolate Chip

Ingradients:-

Certainly! Here are the ingredients typically used to make Butter Chicken, a popular Indian sweetdish:

For the Mint Chocolate :

2 cups heavy cream 1 cup whole milk 3/4 cup granulated sugar 2 teaspoons peppermint extract (for mint flavor) Green food coloring (optional, for color) 1 cup chocolate chips or chunks (semisweet or dark chocolate) These ingredients are combined, with the exception of the chocolate chips, and then churned and frozen. The chocolate chips are usually added towards the end of the churning process or mixed into the ice cream after it's churned to evenly distribute them throughout the minty ice cream. Enjoy your Mint Chocolate Chip ice cream!
