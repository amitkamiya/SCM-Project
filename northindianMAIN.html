For the Paratha Dough:

2 cups whole wheat flour (atta) Water (as needed for kneading) 1/2 teaspoon salt 1 tablespoon cooking oil or ghee (clarified butter) For the Filling (optional, for stuffed parathas): Mashed potatoes (for Aloo Paratha) OR OR For other stuffed parathas, you can use a variety of ingredients like: Spiced minced vegetables (such as cauliflower, spinach, or radish) Minced meat (such as chicken, lamb, or beef) Grated cheese (for cheese paratha) Paneer (Indian cottage cheese) with spices (for paneer paratha) Cooked lentils (for dal paratha) For Cooking: Cooking oil or ghee (clarified butter) for frying For Serving (optional): Yogurt Pickles Chutneys
