UPMA


Ingredients:-

 1/2 cup rava (sooji),1 medium sized onion chopped roughly,1 and 1/4 cups water,2 tablespoon milk optional,9 pieces cashews,salt to taste

To temper:

 2 teaspoon oil,1 teaspoon ghee,1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds,1/2 teaspoon urad dal,1/2 teaspoon chana dal,a small sprig curry leaves,1 piece green chilli chopped roughly,1/2 teaspoon ginger chopped finely

IDLI

 1 cup regular rice + 1 cup parboiled rice or 2 cups idli rice or 2 cups parboiled rice
