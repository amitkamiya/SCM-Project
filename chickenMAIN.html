CHICKEN ITEMS

BUTTER CHICKEN

Ingradients:-

For the chicken and marinade:

1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into roughly 1-inch cubes, 1/4 cup full-fat plain yogurt, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste, 1 teaspoon ground coriander seed, 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Lal Mirch, 1 teaspoon cumin, Salt, to taste.

For the sauce:

8 to 10 whole almonds, 8 to 10 whole cashews, 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided, 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1 (15-ounce) can diced tomatoes, 2 tablespoons ground coriander seed, 1 1/2 teaspoons ground garam masala, 1/2 teaspoon ground Kashmiri Lal Mirch, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1 cup water, 1/4 cup heavy cream, 1/2 tablespoon Kasuri Meth.

GRAVY CHICKEN

INGREDIENTS FOR CHICKEN GRAVY:-

Chicken - 1 kg with bone cut into small pieces, Chilli Powder - 1 tblspn, Turmeric Powder - 2 tsp, Garam Masala Powder - 2 tsp, Salt to taste, Yogurt / Curd - ½ cup, Lemon Juice - 2 tblspn, Ginger Garlic Paste - 1 tblspn.

For Masala:

Oil - 5 tblspn, Fennel Seeds / Sombu / Saunf - 1 tblspn, Cumin Seeds / Jeerakam - 1 tsp, Cinnamon / Pattai - 2 cm piece, Onion - 4 large sliced thinly, Capsicum - 1 large sliced thinly, Tomatoes - 4 large sliced thinly, Ginger Garlic Paste - 2 tblspn, Chilli Powder - 1 tblspn, Kashmiri Chilli Powder - 3 tblspn, Coriander Powder - 2 tblspn, Turmeric Powder / Manjal Podi - 1 tsp, Garam Masala Powder - 1 tblspn, Tomato Ketchup - ¼ cup, Green Chilli Sauce - 2 tblspn, Soy Sauce - 1 tblspn, Salt to taste, Coriander leaves a big handful finely chopped.

FRIED CHICKEN

Ingredients For Fried Chicken:-

4 cups all-purpose flour, divided, 2 tablespoons garlic salt, 1 tablespoon paprika, 3 teaspoons pepper, divided, 2-1/2 teaspoons poultry seasoning, 2 large eggs, 1-1/2 cups water,, 1 teaspoon salt 2 broiler/fryer chickens (3-1/2 to 4 pounds each), cut up, Oil for deep-fat frying.

TANDOORI CHICKEN

Ingredients for Tandoori Chicken:-

1 teaspoon ground cumin 1 teaspoon ground turmeric 1 teaspoon cayenne 1 tablespoon garam masala 1 tablespoon sweet (not hot) paprika 1 cup plain yogurt (can sub buttermilk) 2 tablespoons lemon juice 4 garlic cloves, minced 2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger 1 teaspoon salt 4 whole chicken leg quarters (drumsticks and thighs), skinless, bone-in
